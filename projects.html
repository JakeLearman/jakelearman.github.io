Optimising Solutions To The Engima Machine

For my dissertation, I set myself the aim of cracking the Enigma machine by taking advantage of modern hardware capabilities. In order to do this, I first implemented a purely functional Enigma machine in Haskell and cracked it using the method found at Bletchley Park. At this stage I designed a brtue force solution of cracking Enigma which involved comparing every possible encryption of every letter against the encrypted phrase and some suspected plain-text.
